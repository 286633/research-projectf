I have created this webstite with the skills I have learned in Web Design this year. In this website I will be covering some great spots to fish at and some not so great spots in Illinois.



About

In Illinois there are some great places to fish at. I will be covering some of the good places and some of the bad places. Your best luck is to go for bass and bluegill. There are other fish that are good too. If you are looking to catch a personal best or just catch a lot of fish, I would recommend fishing for bass. If you are looking for fish to eat I would recommend fishing for bluegill. There are other fish that are decently populated like catfish and trout.

I will be telling you about the Skokie Lagoons, Beck Lake, Belleau Lake, and Glenview Lake. This website will tell you the best spots to fish at the the best fish to fish for. It will also tell you details about the lake and how to catch the best fish. You will learn all about these lakes around Illinois and you'll be catching tons of fish the text time you go.
