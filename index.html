Fishing in Illionois

In Illinois there are some great places to fish at. I will be covering some of the good places and some of the bad places. Your best luck is to go for bass and bluegill. There are other fish that are good too. If you are looking to catch a personal best or just catch a lot of fish, I would recommend fishing for bass. If you are looking for fish to eat I would recommend fishing for bluegill. There are other fish that are decently populated like catfish and trout.
